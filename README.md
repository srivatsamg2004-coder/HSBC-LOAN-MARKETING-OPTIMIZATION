# HSBC-LOAN-MARKETING-OPTIMIZATION
HSBC Bank Personal Loan Acceptance Analysis
HSBC Bank Personal Loan Acceptance Analysis & Marketing Optimization
Project Overview
This project analyzes customer data from HSBC Bank to identify the factors that influence personal loan acceptance. Using Microsoft Excel, an interactive dashboard was developed to help the marketing team target the right customers, improve campaign effectiveness, increase conversion rates, and reduce unnecessary marketing costs.
________________________________________
Business Problem
Previous marketing campaigns targeted a broad customer base, resulting in low loan acceptance rates and higher campaign costs.
The objective of this project is to identify customer segments that are more likely to accept personal loan offers and provide data-driven recommendations for future marketing campaigns.
________________________________________
Objectives
•	Analyze customer demographics and financial characteristics. 
•	Identify key factors influencing loan acceptance. 
•	Build an interactive Excel dashboard. 
•	Improve marketing efficiency through customer segmentation. 
•	Generate actionable business insights.

________________________________________
Key Business Insights
•	Customers with higher annual income showed significantly higher loan acceptance rates. 
•	Highly educated customers were more likely to accept personal loan offers. 
•	Customers with larger families demonstrated greater interest in personal loan products. 
________________________________________
Strategic Recommendations
•	Focus marketing campaigns on high-income customer segments. 
•	Design personalized loan offers for highly educated customers. 
•	Use customer segmentation to improve campaign efficiency and reduce acquisition costs.
